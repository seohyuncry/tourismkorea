Traditional Open Market Tour

Traditional Open Market Tour

Gyeongdong Herb Market, the starting point of the traditional market tour, supplies 70% of oriental medicine raw materials and is famous for having more than 1,000 related stores and oriental medicine hospitals. And the Garak-dong Agricultural Produce Market, the oldest and largest seafood and agricultural products wholesale market in Korea, is located in Songpa-gu, Seoul. Lastly, experience the colorful market culture by visiting Dongdaemun Market, which was the center of Korea's textile industry in the 1970s and is now the center of Korea's wholesale and retail fashion industry.

Duration: 4 Hour(s) - 30 Minute(s)

Tour Category: Traditional Market Shopping

contact us

Package Itinerary

  • Gyeongdong Herb Market → Fruit Wholesale Market → East Gate Textile Market

  • After hotel pickup, visit the Gyeongdong Market, which is one of the largest herbal medicine and ginseng markets in South Korea. Supplying 70% of the nation's herbal medicine ingredients, the market also sells agricultural products and fish. The market also has more than 1,000 related shops and oriental medical clinics in the area.

  • Gyeongdong Market

  • Fruit Wholesale Market

  • Your next stop would be at the oldest and largest wholesale market in Korea for marine and agricultural products. The Garak-dong Agricultural Market, which opened in 1985, is located in the Songpa-gu district of Seoul. The market offers independent sections for fresh fruits, vegetables, meat, and fish in a large area spanning around 543,450 square meters.

  • Lastly, explore the vibrant Dongdaemun Market where you'll find an interesting mix of tradition and modernity. The market became the heart of the Korean textile industry in the 1970s and has further developed to become the center of Korea's wholesale and retail fashion industries. The Dongdaemun Shopping and Town Center Complex offers something for everyone. From materials such as burlap, faux, furs, linen, and silk, to beads and precious stones for making jewelry.

  • Textile Market
