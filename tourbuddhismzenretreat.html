Korean Buddhism Zen Retreat 12 Days Tour 11 Nights

Korean Buddhism Zen Retreat 12 Days Tour 11 Nights

Experience Korea’s Buddhist culture through a 12-day, 11-night Korean Buddhist Zen tour! Starting from Jogyesa Temple, temple life programs are held at Bongeunsa Temple, Naksansa Temple, etc. allowing you to fully experience Korea's Buddhist culture. Program contents include temple tours, tea ceremonies, and meditation. You can also experience mask dancing at Andong Hahoe Village, and in Busan, you can visit Haeundae Beach and Jagalchi Market.

Duration: 12 Day(s) - 11 Night(s)

Tour Category: Korean Buddhism Tour

1ST DAY ARRIVAL - SEOUL

Arrive at ICN airport and welcomed by our representative. Transfer to hotel for free at leisure.

2ND DAY - SEOUL

Morning: Jogyesa Temple’s ‘Global Inspiration’ Temple Tour Single Pillar Gate - Exterior of the Main Buddha Hall - Interior of the Main Buddha Hall - Bell Pavilion - Stupa

Lunch: "Baru Gongyang", formal monastic meal.

Afternoon: 2.5 hour Temple Life program at Bongeunsa Temple, the head temple of the SEON Buddhism - This program is for those who want a brief experience of both Korean and Buddhist culture. Basic programs include the temple tour, tea ceremony, meditation, and making lotus lanterns. Afterwards, visit COEX (Korea convention & exhibition center) shopping mall.

Overnight in Seoul

  • Jogyesa

  • Temple

  • Bongeunsa

3RD DAY SEOUL - YONGMUNSA- SHINHEUNGSA, MT. SEORAK

Morning: Yongmunsa Temple in Yongmun Mountain, founded in AD 913 during Shilla Dynasty, National Treasure #531

Afternoon: Shinheungsa Temple, founded in AD652 during Shilla Dynasty.

Overnight in Mt. Seorak

  • Yongmunsa

  • Sinheungsa

  • Seoraksan

4TH DAY MT. SEORAK- NAKSANSA- WOLJEONGSA TEMPLE STAY

Morning: Naksansa, found in AD 671 during Shilla dynasty

Afternoon: Woljeonsa Temple Stay program starts at 3pm.

Overnight Woljeonsa, MT. Odae

  • Naksansa

  • Buddhism

  • Woljeongsa

5TH DAY MT. ODAE - ANDONG

Transfer to Andong Hahoe Village which Village has conserved Hahoe Byeolsingut Mask Dance performed by the public in general, and Seonyujulbul Nori-a boat ride and fire play enjoyed by the nobility called Yang ban. The village conserves many cultural heritages which show Korean traditional living cultures and ancient architectural styles. Visit Mask museum.

Overnight in Andong

  • Hahoe Byeolsingut Mask Dance

  • Andong Hahoe Village

  • Andong

6TH DAY - GYEONGJU

Visit Dosan Confucian Academy - Sacred place of Korean Spiritual Culture

Afternoon: Transfer to Gyeongju and visit Gyeongju National Museum - Free at leisure.

Overnight in Gyeongju

  • Gyeongju

  • Gyeongju National Museum

  • Dosan Confucian Academy

7TH DAY GYEONGJU

Full day Gyeongju tour: Bulguksa Temple, one of Korea's best well-known temples; Seokguram Grotto, one of the Asia's finest Buddhist shrines surrounded by Bodhisattvas and guardian deities; Royal Tumuli Park, displaying a collection of royal Shilla tombs; Anapji Pond, where royal families relaxed and enjoyed themselves; Cheomseongdae Observatory, the world’s earliest known existing observatory.

Overnight in Gyeongju

  • Bulguksa

  • Seokguram

  • Cheomseongdae

8TH DAY GYEONGJU - TONGDOSA- BEOMEOSA- BUSAN

Morning: Tongdosa Temple, founded in AD 646, one of the Three Jewel Temples of Korea; Famous for not having any Buddha statue at the main temple, instead the real shrines of the Sakyamuni Buddha, SARI, are preserved in it.

Afternoon: Beomeosa Temple, founded in AD 678, got its name “heavenly fish at golden well” from a l egend that golden-colored fish had lived in a well filled with golden colored water that never dried up.

Overnight in Busan

  • Gyeongju

  • Beomeosa

  • Busan

9TH DAY BUSAN - HAEINSA TEMPLE- MT. JIRI

Morning: Haeundae Beach & Jagalchi Fish Market

Afternoon: Haeinsa Temple, UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site, home of the Tripitaka Koreana, the most complete collection of Buddhist texts, engraved on 80,000 woodblocks between AD1237 and AD1248.Transfer to Mt. Jiri National Park, where the time stops and nature begins.

Overnight in Mt. Jiri

  • Jagalchi Fish Market

  • Haeinsa

  • Haeundae Beach

10TH DAY MT. JIRI - SEOUL

Morning: Hwaomsa Temple, founded in AD 544 by a monk from India during Baekjae Dynasty. The name Hwaom comes from the ‘Avatam Saka Su tra’ or Flower Garland Sutra in English, one of the most influential Mahayana Sutras of East Asian Buddhism. Cross country drive to Seoul.

  • Hwaomsa

  • Jirisan

  • Buddhism

11TH DAY SEOUL

Free Day. *Optional Tour Available

12TH DAY SEOUL - DEPARTURE

Transfer to Incheon Int’l airport & Departure
