Petite France & Nami Island Tour

Petite France & Nami Island Tour

After being picked up in the morning from your Seoul hotel, we head to our first destination, Petite France. It is also famous as a photo spot with its colorful buildings surrounded by a valley. You can also visit the museum dedicated to Antoine de Saint-Exu`péry and his works. Then, we visit Nami Island and take a tour of the imaginary country ‘Naminara Republic’. In addition, we will take a walk along the road that was the filming location for Winter Sonata. You can also visit Chuncheon Myeongdong and experience the atmosphere of Myeongdong with lunch.

Duration: 8 Hour(s) - 0 Minute(s)

Tour Category: Full Day Tours

contact us

Package Itinerary

  • Petite France → Nami Island → Chuncheon Myoungdong (Lunch Included)

  • After an early morning pickup from your Seoul hotel, you’ll travel by comfortable air-conditioned coach to Petite France. You will see colorful buildings surrounded by the valley. Great for taking photos. Also, have a small museum about Antoine de Saint-Exupéry and his works. Small gift shop to buy souvenirs and books. If you want to stay here, they also can provide the rooms for you.

  • Petite France

  • Nami Island

  • After that, we will head to Nami Island and catch the ferry. Upon arrival, you’ll enter the ‘Naminara Republic’ – an imaginary country that has invented its own passport, currency, stamp, and telephone card! – and then start your tour. The half-moon-shaped island was named after General Nami, who won an important victory in the 15th century and is buried here.

    Follow your knowledgeable guide as you check out locations where the main characters of Winter Sonata shared the screen and won the hearts of their fans. Released in Korea in 2002, Winter Sonata (Gyeoul Yeonga) quickly became one of the most popular shows on Korean television. You’ll stroll along tree-lined paths where the main couple, Yu-jin and Jun-sang, had some of their most memorable moments. Don't miss this tour if you’re a fan of the show!

    Check out Winter Sonata shots reproduced on poster-size signs throughout Nami Island at locations where the scenes were filmed. Snap photos with the statue of the star-crossed lovers in the center of the island – a fun souvenir!
