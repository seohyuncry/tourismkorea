Touch Of Korea - 5 Days 4 Nights Tour

Korean food is rich in nutrition, well-balanced, and low in calories. It is said you can eat as much Korean food as you like and never gain weight. Widely recognized for its unique flavor and spicy taste, Korean food can easily become the safest and healthiest addiction one can enjoy for life.

1ST DAY ARRIVAL - SEOUL

Arrive at Incheon International Airport & greeted by our representative. Transfer to Seoul and hotel check-in.
Overnight in Seoul. Seoul is a city of palaces, with five huge palace complexes located throughout the city and now restored to their former glory. The city is filled with a wide range of tourist attractions of all types, from outdoor adventures.

2ND DAY SEOUL - NAMI ISLAND - MT. SEORAK

Transfer to Mt. Seorak which is the most beautiful mountain in Korea, via Nami Island which was a shooting place for the Korean TV drama "Winter Sonata".

Tour of Seorak National Park - Gwongeumseong Castle by cable car and visit Sinhungsa temple. Visit Daepo Fishery market.

Overnight in Mt. Seorak hotel.

  • Mt. Seorak

  • Sinhungsa

  • Gwongeumseong

3RD DAY MT. SEORAK - SUWON KOREAN FOLK VILLAGE - SEOUL

Transfer to Suwon and visit Korean Folk Village which was opened on the 3rd October 1974, as an open -air folk museum and international tourist attraction for both Korean and foreign visitors. It is the home of the true Korean heritage where many features of the Korean culture have been collected and preserved for Succeeding generations to see and learn about.

Overnight Seoul

  • Suwon

  • Suwon Korean Folk Village

  • Mt. Seorak

4TH DAY SEOUL

Cheongwadae Sarangchae (Guest house at Presidential house) is a place filled with hope and joy. You can learn about past presidents and the history of the capital, Seoul.

Gyeongbokgung Palace. As the oldest and grandest of Seoul's royal palaces, Gyeongbokgung Palace is a masterpiece of Korean traditional architecture and one of Seoul's most visited locations.

National Folk Museum is exhibiting the religious rituals, customs, food, and social dynamics of traditional Korean Life.

Jogyesa Temple is the chief temple of the Jogye order which represents Korean Buddhism.

Move to Insadong Antique Street and visit major galleries, enjoy free shopping.

Overnight in Seoul

  • Gyeongbokgung

  • Insadong Antique Street

  • Cheongwadae Sarangchae

Transfer to Incheon International Airport & Departure
