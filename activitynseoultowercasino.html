N Seoul Tower & Casino Tour

The tour begins with a visit to N Seoul Tower. Boasting 236.7 meters, the tower is so tall that it offers visitors incredible views of Seoul and the surrounding area. The view is said to offer breathtaking views of the city. You can enjoy a variety of games at Seven Luck Casino in Korea, which is considered like Las Vegas in the United States. And the shopping district located in the center of Gangnam is an ideal place for sightseeing and shopping.

Package Itinerary

  • N Seoul Tower → Seven Luck Casino → Shopping District

  • The tour begins with a visit to N Seoul Tower. Already 236.7 meters tall, the tower sits atop Namsan Mountain, which is approximately 243 meters above sea level. It is so tall that it offers visitors amazing views of Seoul and the surrounding area. The views are so beautiful that many people consider it the best tower in Asia. While here, guests are encouraged to try the N Grill Western-style revolving restaurant (rotating every 48 minutes) and the artistically decorated Sky Restroom. Both offer breathtaking views of the city.

  • N Seoul Tower

  • Seven Luck Casino

  • Next, we'll try our luck at the Seven Luck Casino. Considered as the Korean counterpart of USA's Las Vegas, the casino is where you can enjoy a great variety of gaming options such as Blackjack, Baccarat, Caribbean Poker, among others. Since it's located in the central Gangnam area, the casino is also an ideal place to go sightseeing and shop.
